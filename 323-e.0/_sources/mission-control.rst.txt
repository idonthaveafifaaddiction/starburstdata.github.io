.. _mission-control:

***************
Mission Control
***************

Mission Control is a web-based management console for Starburst Enterprise
Presto. It allows you to to easily create and manage Presto :ref:`data
sources<mc-data-source>`, the :ref:`clusters<mc-cluster>` using the data sources
and the :ref:`users<mc-user>` allowed to manage everything in Mission Control.
