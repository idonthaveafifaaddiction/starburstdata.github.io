    Configure your AWS account #

    You need to configure a cross-account role for your AWS account. This enables the Starburst Galaxy platform (SGP) to deploy and manage Starburst clusters in your account.

    Manually create a cross account role #

    Before you begin, confirm your account manager provides your unique identifiers:

    • Starburst Galaxy account ID
    • External ID

    Create a cross-account role manually from the AWS management console.

    1. In your AWS management console, go to IAM services
    2. Select Roles from the sidebar, click Create account
    3. In Select type of trusted entity, click Another AWS account
    4. In the Account ID field, enter your Starburst Galaxy account ID, provided by your account manager.
    5. Select the Require external ID checkbox and paste your unique External ID provided by your account manager, in the field.
    6. Click Next > Permissions
    7. Click Next > Tags
    8. Click Next > Review
    9. In the Role name field, enter a role name unique to SGP
    10. Click Create role

    Attach an access policy to the cross-account role #

    Attach a new access policy to the cross-account role you created for SGP.

    Before you begin, confirm your account manager provides you with the SGP access policy.

    1. From the list of roles, select the role you created for Starburst Galaxy
    2. On the Permissions tabs, click Add inline policy
    3. From the Policy editor, select the JSON tab
    4. Paste the SGP access policy into Policy editor
      • Your account manager provides this access policy to you
    5. Click Review policy
    6. In the Name, enter a unique policy name and click Create policy

    IMPORTANT: From the Role summary, copy the ROLE ARN.

    Create a hosted zone #

    Create a hosted zone configuration to provide SGP with the following:

    • Name-server values
    • Hosted zone ID
    • EC2 keypair name
