Configure your AWS account

Grant Starburst Galaxy access to your AWS account using a cross-account role. Enabling these permissions allows you to create, edit, and delete Presto clusters from Starburst Galaxy.

Manually create a cross account role

Create a cross-account role manually from the AWS management console.

Before you begin, confirm your account manager provides your unique:

  • Starburst Galaxy account ID
  • External ID
  1. In your AWS management console, go to IAM services
  2. Select Roles from the sidebar, click Create account
  3. In Select type of trusted entity, click Another AWS account
  4. In the Account ID, enter your Starburst Galaxy account ID
    • Your account manager provides this ID to you
  5. Select the Require external ID checkbox and paste your unique External ID in the field
    • Your account manager provides this ID to you
  6. Click Next > Permissions
  7. Click Next > Tags
  8. Click Next > Review
  9. In the Role name field, enter a role name unique to Starburst Galaxy
  10. Click Create role

Attach an access policy to the cross-account role

Attach a new access policy to the cross-account role you created for Starburst Galaxy.

Before you begin, confirm your account manager provides you with the Starburst Galaxy access policy.

  1. From the list of roles, select the role you created for Starburst Galaxy
  2. On the Permissions tabs, click Add inline policy
  3. From the Policy editor, select the JSON tab
  4. Paste the Starburst Galaxy access policy into Policy editor
    • Your account manager provides this access policy to you
  5. Click Review policy
  6. In the Name, enter a unique policy name and click Create policy

IMPORTANT: From the Role summary, copy the ROLE ARN.
