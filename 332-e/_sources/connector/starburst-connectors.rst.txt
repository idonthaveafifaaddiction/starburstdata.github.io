=============================
Starburst Connectors Overview
=============================

Starburst Enterprise Presto includes numerous connectors. These connectors are
either extensions of Presto connectors adding features such as table statistics,
user impersonation and others or completely separate additional connectors for
other data sources.

Connectors and connector extensions, that do **not** require a license:

* :doc:`/connector/starburst-generic-jdbc`
* :doc:`/connector/starburst-hive`
* :doc:`/connector/starburst-hive-security`
* :doc:`/connector/starburst-hive-azurestorage`
* :doc:`/connector/starburst-hive-ibm-cos`
* :doc:`/connector/starburst-kafka` 
* :doc:`/connector/starburst-mysql`
* :doc:`/connector/starburst-postgresql`
* :doc:`/connector/starburst-sqlserver`

Connectors and connector extensions, that require a :doc:`license
