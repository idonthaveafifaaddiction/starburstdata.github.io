This chapter describes the connectors available in Presto to access data
from different data sources.

Starburst Connectors and Connector Extensions
=============================================

Starburst Enterprise Presto includes numerous connectors. These connectors are
either extensions of Presto connectors adding features or completely separate
additional connectors. More information is available in the :doc:`connector
details page </connector/starburst-connectors>`.
