***************
Mission Control
***************

Mission Control is a web-based management console for Starburst Enterprise
Presto. It allows you to to easily create and manage Presto :doc:`data
sources </mission-control/data-source-management>`, the :doc:`clusters </mission-control/cluster-management>` using the data sources
and the :doc:`users </mission-control/user-management>` allowed to manage everything in Mission Control.
