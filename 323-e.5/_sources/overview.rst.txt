********
Overview
********

Presto is a distributed SQL query engine designed to query large data sets
distributed over one or more heterogeneous data sources.

Presto is maintained by the large community of contributors and users of the
`Presto Software Foundation <https://prestosql.io>`_.

Starburst Enterprise Presto is the commercial distribution of Presto, which
includes additional security features, more connectors, a cost-based query
optimizer, support for running additional deployment platforms and much more.

Starburst Enterprise Presto is maintained by
`Starburst <https://www.starburstdata.com>`_.
